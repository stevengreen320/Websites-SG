Teams Stats: Season 2018 - 2019

Graphs Summary

The first graph on the left is allowing you to see which team made the most points for Season 2018 - 2019. You are also able to know the number of wins versus number of losses when the teams were playing at their home city/ stadium.

The second graph give you a comparison of each of the team's home wins versus losses.

The map allows you to identify the eastern and western teams. The arena and stadium capacity is also defined by the marker colors. Blue is arenas with capacity over 20,000. Red is arenas with capacity that is between 20,000 and 18,500 and white is arenas with capacity that is 18,500 and below.
