Players Stats: Season 2018 - 2019

Graphs Summary

PPP is the points per possession for a player. It's a calculation that provides an average of how many points an offensive player averages when he has possession of the ball. The formula is [pts/fga+ (.44 *fta) +to] and a link that can explain this in greater detail is here: https://www.nba.com/.element/media/2.0/teamsites/thunder/statlab-en-october-1819-update.pdf

Player impact estimate is a formula that's used to quantify a player's impact over the entire game and then averaged over the entire season. The formula is (PTS + FGM + FTM – FGA – FTA + Deff.REB + Off.REB/2 + AST + STL + BLK/2 – PF – TO) / (Game.PTS + Game.FGM + Game.FTM – Game.FGA – Game.FTA + Game.Deff.REB + Game.Off.REB/2 + Game.AST + Game.STL + Game.BLK/2 – Game.PF – Game.TO) and more information can be found here: https://www.nbastuffer.com/analytics101/player-impact-estimate-pie/ and here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tbp9P32lyCk
