Summary: Latitude vs X

This project was designed to measure different weather patterns for a ranmdomized selection of places taken from the OpenWeatherAPI app

After the data was pulled, we then used Matplotlib to make charts further expanding zeroing on different locations close to the eqautor or 0 degrees latitude. We made charts displaying Latitude vs Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed. To view these in greater detail, either click on the dropdown at the top or click the picture.
